ASCE 7-16 Minimum Load Compliance Check

Per ASCE 7-16 Section 27.1.5, the design wind load for the MWFRS of an enclosed building must meet minimum standard thresholds:

  • Minimum Wall Pressure: 16.000 psf applied vertically to wall faces.
  • Minimum Roof Pressure: 8.000 psf applied to flat roof projections.
