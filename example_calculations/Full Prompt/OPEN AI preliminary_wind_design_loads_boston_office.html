3. Velocity Pressure at Mean Roof Height

For Exposure C, ASCE 7-16 velocity pressure exposure coefficient at z = 15 ft:

Kz = 2.01(z / zg)^(2/α)
Kz = 2.01(15 / 900)^(2 / 9.5)
Kz = 0.849

Now calculate qh:

qh = 0.00256(0.849)(1.0)(0.85)(1.0)(120²)

Wall Pressure Sign Convention

  • Positive pressure acts toward the surface.
  • Negative pressure is suction acting away from the surface.
  • For the windward wall, the larger inward pressure governs when GCpi = -0.18.
  • For leeward and side walls, the larger outward suction governs when GCpi = +0.18.
