9   Notes & Limitations

Standard: All calculations per ASCE 7-16 (not ASCE 7-22). Chapter 27 Directional Procedure, MWFRS only. Results are not applicable to Component & Cladding (C&C) design — see ASCE 7-16 Chapter 30 for C&C pressures, which will be higher in corner and edge zones.

Roof zones: Zone distances are measured horizontally from the windward wall edge. For this building, 2h = 30 ft = L, so Zone 3 has no tributary area. Only Zones 1 and 2 apply.

Windward wall: Uses qz evaluated at height z; for this single-story building z = h = 15 ft throughout, so qz = qh. For taller multi-story buildings, qz varies with height per Table 26.10-1.

Internal pressure: GCpi = +0.18 governs roof uplift and leeward/side wall suction (Case A). GCpi = −0.18 governs windward wall pressure (Case B). Both cases must be checked and the maximum used for design of each element.

Minimum pressure: §27.4.7 minimum of 16 psf net lateral is satisfied (29.43 psf > 16 psf) for both internal pressure cases.
