11. Exposure Category Note

This calculation assumes Exposure C, which may be conservative for parts of Boston depending on the actual site surroundings.

If the building is located in dense urban terrain and meets the code requirements for Exposure B, the velocity pressure may be lower. For example, using an approximate Exposure B value at 15 ft:

qh = 0.00256(0.57)(1.0)(0.85)(1.0)(120²)

This is roughly a 33% reduction compared with the Exposure C value of 26.6 psf. However, Exposure B should not be assumed unless the surrounding terrain qualifies in all relevant wind directions.
